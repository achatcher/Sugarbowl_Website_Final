About Us

Kalamazoo's Oldest Neighborhood Dive Bar. The music is loud. The drinks are strong. The food is messy and amazing. The SugarBowl has been around since the ’40s, and we’re proud of it. Punk, metal, blues, country — if it rattles the walls, it belongs here. We’re for the weird, the loud, the misfits, the metalheads, the punks, the people who just want a good time. Basically, if you’re not a jerk, you fit in. Even if you think you don’t, you probably do. Live shows, DJs, karaoke that actually rocks — sometimes all at once. That’s the point, right?
